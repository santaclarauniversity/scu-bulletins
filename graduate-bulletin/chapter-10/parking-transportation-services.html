              Parking & Transportation Services

              An SCU parking permit allows vehicles to park in designated lots/garages according to the customer’s parking permit category. SCU does not guarantee customers will find parking in their desired parking lot or parking space. Customers who are unable to find parking in their designated lot(s)/garage(s) may park in any “F” overflow lots.

              During parking permit enforcement hours (Monday-Friday, 6 a.m.-8 p.m.), all vehicles on campus must have an SCU-issued parking permit displayed. Permit hours are posted at each parking lot/garage. Guests may park in any “B,” “E,” “F,” or “visitor” marked spaces on Saturdays andSundays without a parking permit.
