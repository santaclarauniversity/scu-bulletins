              Chapter 6
              MBA Curriculum

              Online Programs

              The Leavey School of Business offers several online programs. These programs include Online MBA, Online MS in Marketing, Online MS in Finance and Analytics, and Online MS in Business Analytics. Online programs maintain identical admission requirements, tuition, learning objectives, degree requirements, faculty, policies, and procedures as their traditional on-campus counterparts. The online platform accommodates a wider range of students and enhances our capacity to deliver advanced digital education.

              Courses in these online programs are asynchronous. This education format allows students to study at their own pace and provides flexibility to their own schedule through onlinelearning.scu.edu.

              Admissions

              The admission requirements for online programs closely resemble those outlined for on-campus programs (see Chapter 2), with the exception that online program applicants must be residents within the United States where SCU is authorized to operate (refer to authorized states).

              Prospective students can submit their applications at http://slate.scu.edu/apply/. Currently, online programs are unable to admit students on an F1 visa.

              New cohorts for the Online MBA commence in the Fall, Winter, Spring, and Summer terms. The Online MS programs accept new cohorts only in the Fall and Spring terms. For the Online MS Finance & Analytics and Online MS Business Analytics programs, proficiency in college-level calculus and statistics is required as prerequisites.

              Student Success Seminar

              Prior to starting the program, students are encouraged to enroll in the Student Success Seminar. This is an ungraded, open course designed to familiarize them with the online learning platform. It is strongly advised that students complete this seminar before embarking on their courses. The seminar establishes success expectations and assists students in formulating their success strategies. Access to this course is provided through onlinelearning.scu.edu.

              Residency Requirement

              Each online program offered by the Leavey School incorporates an in-person residency at the program's outset, aimed at providing orientation, networking opportunities, career workshops, community service activities, in-person coursework, and immersion experiences in Silicon Valley and at Santa Clara University. The specific manifestation of these elements varies according to the requirements of each program. Given that all online program residencies occur over the same weekend, faculty and students have the opportunity to interact across programs and disciplines. Ultimately, the residency facilitates personal connections for online students, fostering a sense of community. Feedback from previous online students at LSB consistently highlights the relationships formed during the residency as a pivotal factor in feeling connected to their cohorts and grasping the essence of "being a bronco."

              The Online MBA program mandates a 4-day on-campus residency, featuring a MGMT 3000 course component. Similarly, the Online MS Marketing program entails a 3-day residency, incorporating MGMT 3200 courses. The Online MS Finance & Analytics program requires a 3-day residency, including an FNCE 2400 and MSIS 2402 Bootcamp. Lastly, the Online MS Business Analytics program necessitates a 3-day residency, encompassing an MSIS 2402 and IDIS 3802 Bootcamp. Residency is hosted the weekend before the term starts and consists of pre and post-course work.

              Program Differentiator

              Online MBA: Silicon Valley Dashboard

              Crafted to transform everyday professionals into Silicon Valley Professionals (SVPs), the Online MBA program offers a curriculum centered on innovation and ethical leadership. As students embark on their SVP journey, they'll have the opportunity to monitor their advancement through the Silicon Valley Professional dashboard. This comprehensive portfolio empowers students to align their elective courses and co-curricular experiences with their career goals., These experiences encourage ongoing reflection on their professional development throughout the program.

              The dashboard serves as a networking hub, facilitating interactions with instructors, peers, and mentors. It also offers a platform for students to seek and receive feedback on their continuous progress.

              Online MS Marketing: MarTech Focus

              Throughout the Online MS Marketing program, students will explore cutting-edge marketing technologies, strategies, and tools. These include customer relationship management (CRM) systems, marketing automation platforms, data analytics tools, and emerging technologies. Students use these tools to gather, analyze, and interpret data, informing marketing decisions and crafting comprehensive strategies. The tools provided allow students to develop a thorough understanding of how technology influences and improves contemporary marketing strategies.

              With a MarTech focus, students will gain hands-on experience with digital marketing tactics such as search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, content marketing, and email marketing, while also learning how to integrate these tactics with broader marketing initiatives.

              Online MS Finance & Analytics: Finance Forward

              In the Online MSFA program, students will acquire more than just cutting-edge finance and analytics skills. Our guiding principle, "Finance Forward," guarantees that students not only master the latest tools but also develop the judgment to select the appropriate tool for each challenge and the confidence to use it effectively.

              In every course, students will face complex business challenges, addressing them with fresh analytical methods. After each course, students will record their insights, which will be assembled into an ePortfolio to be presented at the conclusion of the program. Through each course, students will develop a comprehensive understanding of how these tools interconnect and gain a strategic mindset essential for success in Silicon Valley and beyond.

              Online MS Business Analytics: Analytics Showdown Competition

              The Online MSBA program allows students to participate in a two-week competition. During this competition, they are using actual data from companies within Silicon Valley. They will work with their group members to devise and present solutions to real-world business challenges.

              The Analytics Showdown embodies an essential truth: today's business analysts and data scientists must use basic and sophisticated tools, techniques, and methods to solve business challenges collaboratively. Of course, mastering these tools is essential, but the key to innovation is refining critical thinking skills to determine the appropriate approach for each situation and business challenge.

              The competition will provide students with the opportunity to refine their technical knowledge and programming skills acquired throughout the program, including R and Python. Their groups will present their competition entries to a panel of judges composed of faculty, board members, company executives, and/or alums. Elements of this curriculum theme will be implemented in each course throughout the program and culminate in competition.

              Career Requirement

              To promote career growth and professional advancement, all online graduate programs require that students engage with the SCU Graduate Business Career Management team. This includes participation in designated workshops, career coaching sessions, resume reviews, or completing various career reflections. While enrolled at SCU, students have the flexibility to fulfill these requirements at their convenience. However, they must complete their career requirements by the final quarter to be eligible for graduation.

              Requirements:

              • Meet with a Career Coach within their second quarter
              • Submit two reflection papers after attending each Graduate Business Programs Career Event or watching recordings of Career Management workshops

              GBP Anywhere Program

              The GBP Anywhere Program allows MBA and MS students to enroll in on-campus or online electives from various Graduate Business Programs. This program aims to enhance flexibility and course options for Online and Evening students.

              For additional information, refer to the GBP Anywhere Programs Section in Chapter 6.
