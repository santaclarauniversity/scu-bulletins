              Program Curriculum Overview

              At the Leavey School of Business, we are committed to fostering a dynamic learning environment that nurtures critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills. Our curriculum integrates academic rigor with real-world applications, ensuring that our graduates possess the knowledge, competencies, and confidence necessary to excel in their professional careers.

              Each curriculum is thoughtfully crafted to deliver a comprehensive and enriching educational experience, equipping students with the tools they need to succeed in their chosen field. Core Courses form the foundation of each program, providing students with a robust theoretical framework and essential business fundamentals. Additionally, students have the flexibility to tailor their curriculum by selecting elective courses that align with their interests and career objectives.

              In programs such as MSFA, MSBA, and MSIS, our curriculum prioritizes experiential learning opportunities, including practicum and capstone projects, and internships opportunities. These hands-on experiences allow students to apply their knowledge in real-world contexts and gain invaluable practical skills. Similarly, our MBA curriculum offers an optional global perspective, exposing students to diverse cultures, perspectives, and global issues to prepare them for success in an interconnected world.

              Throughout their academic journey, students receive comprehensive support and resources to aid in their career development. From personalized career counseling to professional development workshops and networking opportunities, we are dedicated to empowering our students to achieve their professional goals.

              For detailed information on program structure, including units and courses, please refer to the respective program sections in Chapter 6.
