              Welcome From the Dean

              WELCOME FROM THE DEAN

              Welcome to the Leavey School of Business, where Silicon Valley Innovation and high-impact experiential learning empower students with the skills to positively impact organizations and society.   Since 1923, we have been shaping innovative and ethical business leaders. Becoming a member of our community means you will collaborate with brilliant minds on and off campus in Silicon Valley, at the forefront of business and education. This unique intersection allows you to immediately apply classroom learning to real-world scenarios.

              At Santa Clara University, our deep traditions of values-based learning and educating the whole person sit at the intersection of business and education. This results in inspired and impactful leadership that instills the principles of ethical business into our students.

              As a Leavey student, you are joining a robust network of successful peers, alumni, faculty, and business leaders spanning industries and generations. These relationships offer valuable connections at every career stage. As a Santa Clara Bronco, you will create immediate bonds with others who have experienced the impact of our programs, forging a lifelong community always within reach.

              For 100 years, the Leavey School of Business at Santa Clara University has educated principled and innovative leaders ready to impact the world. This year, we are launching our Leavey PLUS initiative through which we focus on partnerships, leadership, unity, and sustainability to ensure that our graduates continue to be prepared to transform both their companies and the world at large.

              Whether your goals include personal growth, advancement, a career change, or a new business venture of your own, you are in the right place. Let’s embark on our next century together!

              Ed Grier, Dean

              Leavey School of Business
